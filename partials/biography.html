Biography

After spending a little over a decade in Inside and Outside Sales, I made the move to open my own business, Lucky Dog Digital, LLC working to help small- and medium-sized businesses manage their inbound digital and content marketing.

Through these clients, I got my first taste of frontend web development. It started out as simple changes here and there and placing tracking pixels, however I soon realized that I had a deep passion for the work.

This passion led me to attend Galvanize's Full Stack Program in 2015, and through those six months I was able to move beyond the frontend and further my love of creating apps, building APIs, drawing up Data Models, and scaling my creations.

Now I'm back to building Lucky Dog Digital full time and am beginning to help the business reach its full potential as an a la carte virtual agency.
